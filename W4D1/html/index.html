  • A Little Love

Description

Bacon wrapped hot dogs or better known as "gift of the gods" can typically be found while wandering the sidewalks of San Francisco late at night. The chefs that make these magnificent creations have spents years mastering their culinary abilities.

Directions

  1. Carefully break 4 toothpicks in half. Wrap each hot dog in a strip of bacon and secure with a toothpick half at each end. Set aside.
  2. Bring a large skillet or grill pan sprayed with nonstick spray to medium-high heat on the stove.
  3. Add onion, bell pepper, and jalapeno pepper and cook until softened, about 4 minutes. Remove from the skillet/pan and set aside.
  4. Remove the skillet/pan from heat, re-spray, and bring to medium heat. Add bacon-wrapped hot dogs and cook until bacon is crisp and fully cooked and hot dogs are heated through, about 6 minutes, rotating the hot dogs several times to evenly cook the bacon. Set aside.
  5. Evenly spread mayo inside the buns. Remove toothpicks from the hot dogs and place a bacon-wrapped dog in each bun. Top each dog with a teaspoon of ketchup.
  6. Evenly distribute veggie mixture among the dogs. Eat up!
  7. Enjoy the gift of life
